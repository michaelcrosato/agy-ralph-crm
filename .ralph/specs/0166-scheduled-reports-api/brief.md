# Task 0166: Scheduled Reports & Email Delivery Engine - Brief

## Objective
Establish a high-value Commercial Scheduled Reports & Email Delivery Engine for the CRM Core system under Phase 6. This allows sales representatives, managers, and system administrators to schedule any saved analytical report to be executed on a recurring schedule (daily, weekly, monthly) and sent to specified email recipients.

## Core Value
- **Automated Insights**: Delivers reports automatically without users needing to log into the dashboard.
- **Recipient Dispatching**: Supports distributing performance and sales reports directly to external stakeholders or client recipients.
- **Tenant Context and RLS Protection**: Fully runs under active tenant row-level security isolation, ensuring schedule management and execution logs are private and secure.
